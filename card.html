Tardigrade

Tardigrades can survive in space and have been experimented on in the International Space Station!

  • Scientific Name: phylum Tardigrada
  • Average Length: 1.5mm
  • Average Lifespan: 2.5 years
  • Habitat: Often found on lichens and mosses

Given everything they need to survive, tardigrades live around 2.5 years, but they can also go into a state of suspended animation in which they can withstand drying and temperatures up to -272 degrees. Celcius. They can live up to 30 years in this state of suspension.
